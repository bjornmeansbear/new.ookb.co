The Office of Kristian Bjornard

Publishing & Identity Design.

Baltimore, MD / Minneapolis, MN

Our studio offers identity, design, and programming services. We focus on identity systems, design for books and magazines, and websites for the art, architecture, and other cultural fields.

OOKB’s current focuses revolve around two ideologies: “create anywhere publish everywhere” and “pure content.” CAPE and Pure Content are pieces of a system and set of tools for both print and web publishing (and hopefully more!).

Originally founded in Minneapolis, MN, OOKB is now based in Baltimore, MD. We are a rotating crew of designers, developers, writers, photographers, and vagabonds.
